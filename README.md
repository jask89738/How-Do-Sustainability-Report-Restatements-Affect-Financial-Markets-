# How-Do-Sustainability-Report-Restatements-Affect-Financial-Markets-
Project objectives

Four research goals
01
Core Replication
Verify Hertl & Maniora (2026): do sustainability report restatements (SRS) impair analysts' earnings forecast accuracy?

02
In-depth Extension
Investigate heterogeneous effects of SRS characteristics — restatement type, quantification level, and materiality.

03
Mechanism Testing
Examine whether GRI adoption and external assurance mitigate the negative effects of SRS.

04
Policy Implications
Connect findings to CSRD and emerging frameworks, with implications for regulators and market participants.

Data & variables

Variable design
Category	Variable	Definition	Source
Dependent	Analyst Forecast Error	(Actual EPS − Forecast EPS) / Actual EPS	I/B/E/S, Refinitiv
Independent	Sustainability Restatement (SRS)	Dummy = 1 if firm restated prior sustainability report in year t	Press releases, CSR reports, GRI
Moderator	External Assurance	Dummy = 1 if assured by third party (esp. Big 4)	Assurance statements
Moderator	GRI Standards Adoption	Dummy / application level following GRI	GRI Disclosure Database
Controls	Size, M/B, Analyst following, Earnings vol.	Standard firm-level controls	Compustat, CRSP
Empirical strategy

Analysis workflow
1
Sample
2015–2025, S&P 500 / STOXX 600, excl. financials.

2
Descriptives
Compare forecast errors between SRS vs. non-SRS firms.

3
Baseline
Panel FE model: Forecast Error = β₁·SRS + γ·Controls + μᵢ + λₜ + ε.

4
Heterogeneity
Subgroups by E/S/G, quantitative vs. qualitative, materiality; interaction with assurance.

5
Robustness
Alt. accuracy measures, PSM / IV, placebo tests.

Expected outputs

Core findings
Finding 1
SRS significantly increases analysts' earnings forecast errors.
Finding 2
Negative effects are sharpest for environmental, quantitative, and value-material restatements.
Finding 3
External assurance acts as a governance mechanism that mitigates the negative impact of SRS.
Extensions: Market reactions (CARs around SRS announcements), NLP-based textual analysis of restatement tone, and EU vs. US international comparison.
